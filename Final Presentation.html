Introduction

Dataset is from University of California in Irvine, California

Data is from a Tiawanese bank

30,000 observations

Variables Used

  • Credit (> 0)
  • Age (> 0)
  • Gender (M, F)
  • Education (High School, Uni, Grad School, Other)
  • Marital Status (Single, Married, Other)
  • Current Payment Status (-1, 0, 1, …, 8)
  • Current Bill (>= 0)
  • Previous Bill (>= 0)
  • Default (Yes, No)
